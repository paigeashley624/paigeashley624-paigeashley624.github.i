About Me

I can't say that ever really knew what I wanted to do or be ever since I was a kid. I used to think I wanted to be a forensic scientist until my interest in solving mysteries was met with the realization that I would have to see corpses. Ever since graduating college, I have worked in event management which I liked for some time. The fast pace, variety, and seeing a project through from start to finish was exciting. Over time, though fun, and challenging at times I did not see this as a long-term career for myself. Unfortunately due to Covid-19 the event industry took a pretty hard hit. Fortunately for me though, I was laid off and was quite excited. This gave me the push needed to explore a new career path and reevaluate where I wanted to be.

After months of self study, I decided that programming is the route for me and moved into Software Development with Actualize coding bootcamp. Programming had provided the challenge I needed in addition to the excitement of watching projects come together. Much like events, there is so much excitement that comes with programming and anything is possible. The experience that I gained over the years working in hospitality was enormous and I hope to implement that in my future developer role.
